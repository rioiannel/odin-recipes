Adobo Recipes

Description

Chicken marinated in soy sauce and vinegar

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Pepper
  • Dahon ng Laurel

Steps

  1. Put chicken inside a saucepan
  2. Add soy sauce and vinegar
  3. Cook in medium heat
  4. Once boiled, let it simmer
  5. Add pepper and laurel
