Lumpia Recipes

Description

Minced meat wrapped in Lumpia Wrapper

Ingredients

  • Pork (Minced)
  • Carrots
  • Peas
  • Egg
  • Lumpia Wrapper

Steps

  1. Combine minced meat, eggs, carrots and peas
  2. Cook them in low fire setting
  3. Set aside
  4. Once cooled, wrapped in Lumpia wrapper
  5. Fry and high fire setting
  6. Enjoy!
